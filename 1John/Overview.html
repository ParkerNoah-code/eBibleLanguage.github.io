1 John Lessons

I. Prologue: The Word of Life (1:1–4)

  • 1:1–3 – The Word Revealed
  • 1:4 – Purpose: Fulfilled Joy

II. Walking in the Light (1:5–2:2)

  • 1:5 – God is Light
  • 1:6–7 – Fellowship in the Light
  • 1:8–10 – Confession and Forgiveness
  • 2:1–2 – Christ Our Advocate

III. Knowing God through Obedience and Love (2:3–11)

  • 2:3–6 – Obedience and Imitation
  • 2:7–8 – The New Commandment
  • 2:9–11 – Love and Light

IV. Affirmation and Warning to All Believers (2:12–17)

  • 2:12–14 – Address to Believers
  • 2:15–17 – Do Not Love the World

V. Remaining in the Truth Amid False Teachers (2:18–27)

  • 2:18–21 – Antichrists and the Last Hour
  • 2:22–23 – Denial of the Son
  • 2:24–25 – Abide in the Word
  • 2:26–27 – Taught by the Anointing

VI. Children of God: Hope and Purity (2:28–3:3)

  • 2:28–29 – Confidence in His Coming
  • 3:1–3 – God's Love and Our Hope

VII. Righteous Living vs. Sinful Practice (3:4–10)

  • 3:4–6 – Lawlessness vs. Abiding
  • 3:7–10 – Children of God or the Devil

VIII. Love as the Mark of God’s Children (3:11–24)

  • 3:11–15 – Love vs. Hatred
  • 3:16–18 – Sacrificial Love
  • 3:19–22 – Assurance Before God
  • 3:23–24 – Belief, Love, and the Spirit

IX. Discernment of Spirits and the Spirit of Truth (4:1–6)

  • 4:1–3 – Test the Spirits
  • 4:4–6 – Knowing the Spirit of Truth

X. God is Love: Theology and Practice (4:7–21)

  • 4:7–10 – Love and Atonement
  • 4:11–16 – Perfected Love
  • 4:17–21 – Love and Fearlessness

XI. Faith, Obedience, and the Testimony of God (5:1–12)

  • 5:1–5 – Faith Overcomes the World
  • 5:6–9 – The Triple Witness
  • 5:10–12 – The Testimony of Life

XII. Assurance and Final Exhortations (5:13–21)

  • 5:13–15 – Confidence in Prayer
  • 5:16–17 – Sin Leading to Death
  • 5:18–20 – Born of God, Kept from Evil
  • 5:21 – Guard Yourselves from Idols
