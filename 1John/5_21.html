Lessons > 1 John > Guard Yourselves from Idols

1 John 5:21

21 Τεκνία, φυλάξατε ἑαυτὰ ἀπὸ τῶν εἰδώλων.

Greek Word Parsing / Gloss
Τεκνία Noun, neuter nominative/accusative plural (vocative here), diminutive of τέκνον – “little children” or “dear children”
φυλάξατε Verb, 2nd person plural, aorist imperative active of φυλάσσω – “guard”, “keep”, “watch out for”
ἑαυτά Reflexive pronoun, accusative neuter plural (2nd person) – “yourselves”
ἀπὸ Preposition with genitive – “from”, “away from”
τῶν εἰδώλων Noun with article, neuter genitive plural of εἴδωλον – “idols”, “images”, or “false gods”
