Notes

The major grammatical groups are:

Nouns - First, Second, Third Declension

Verbs - Tense Forms (Present, Imperfect, Future, Aorist, Perfect Pluperfect), Voices (Active, Middle, Passive), Mood (Indicative, Subjunctive, Imperative, Optative)

Prepositions - Often Meaning is Case Dependent

Pronouns - Personal, Relative, Demonstrative, Reflexive, Reciprocal, Interrogative, Indefinite

Adjectives and Adverbs - Modify Nouns or Verbs

Participles and Infinitives - Verbal Adjective or Verbal Noun

The following order is designed to make learning easier and get the learner reading texts sooner:

  • Nouns - First and Second Declension
  • Verbs - Present, Imperfect, Future Active Indicative
    - Contract Verbs
  • Prepositions
  • Pronouns - Personal, Relative
  • Adjectives and Adverbs
  • Verbs - First and Second Aorist Active Indicative
    - Liquid Verbs
  • Nouns - Third Declension
  • Verbs - Perfect, Pluperfect Active Indicative
  • Verbs - Middle, Passive Voice
  • Participles and Infinitives - Present, Aorist, Perfect
  • Pronouns - Demonstrative, Reflexive, Reciprocal, Interrogative, Indefinite
  • Verbs - Subjunctives, Imperatives, Optatives
