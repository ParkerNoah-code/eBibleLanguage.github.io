Contract Verbs

Contract verbs are verbs whose stems end in a vowel (α, ε, or ο) that "contracts" with the thematic vowel of the ending when conjugated. This contraction often alters the spelling of the verb forms. It is important to recognize these patterns of changes to parse contract verbs correctly.
