To Be Forms

Present Active Indicative of εἰμί ("I am")

Person Singular Plural
1st Person εἰμί (I am) ἐσμέν (we are)
2nd Person εἶ (you are) ἐστέ (y’all are)
3rd Person ἐστίν (he/she/it is) εἰσίν (they are)

Imperfect Active Indicative of εἰμί ("I was")

Person Singular Plural
1st Person ἤμην (I was) ἦμεν (we were)
2nd Person ἦσθα (you were) ἦτε (y’all were)
3rd Person ἦν (he/she/it was) ἦσαν (they were)
